# Integrals

An integral accumulates infinitesimal change. In engineering, that can mean total displacement, accumulated charge, or total work along an interval.

## Fundamental theorem of calculus

Part 1 connects the accumulation function to differentiation. Part 2 lets us evaluate a definite integral using any antiderivative.

## u-substitution

When a derivative-like inner expression appears, substitute it with a new variable to simplify the integral.
