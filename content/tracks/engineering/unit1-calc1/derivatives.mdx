# Derivatives

A derivative measures instantaneous change. For engineering systems, derivatives connect position to velocity, velocity to acceleration, and state variables to sensitivity.

## Power rule

If \(f(x)=ax^n\), then \(f'(x)=anx^{n-1}\). Preserve the sign and reduce the exponent only after multiplying by the original power.

## Chain rule

For a composite function \(f(g(x))\), differentiate the outside first, then multiply by \(g'(x)\). This pattern appears constantly in sinusoidal forcing, exponential decay, and nonlinear transformations.
